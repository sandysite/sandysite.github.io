This webpage will teach you Vertical Algorithm.

Vertical Algorith is (in my opinion) the easiest math strategy.
I believe that with enough practice, anyone with a functioning memory can learn this.

STEPS

1: Start by ruling up your table. Here I used two numbers, you can use more than two.

2: Always start from the right. Start by adding up your numbers that are vertical from each other.
In this case, you would add the circled two.

3: Now we go to the next ones. Because it added to over 10, we add the 1 from 10 up here,.
and include it in the addition of these two, making it adding three.

3: This next one adds to more than 10, but there's nowhere to put the remaining 1.
Instead, we will just put the full number instead of the single digit, since there's nowhere to add.
